CAD Conquer

With mouse in your hand & design in mind, get ready for most awaiting event, CAD Conquer. A platform for all the CAD designers to present their fastest designing skills!!

Rules:

  • Max no of participants : 1
  • Round 1
    • Student has to draw a basic 2D&3D CAD diagram given by the coordinators.(will be conducted on 1st day and second day).
    • 1st round will be conducted for different batches,and short-listed candidates from each batch will be going to perform in finals.
    • Batches will be selected for competition on first come first serve basis.
    • Shortly listed candidates from each batch will participate in finals and winners will be selected based on perfection & time.
  • Round 2
    • Only one 3D diagram will be given to the short listed candidates. (This will be conducted on 2nd day)
    • Certificate of Excellence will be given to winners along with cash prize.
    • Certificate of participation will be given to all the participants .

Note: You have a facility to bring your laptop or we will provide you a laptop which contains autocad software.

Registration fee: 400/- per person

For participants who are interested to participate in multiple events, surprise discounts will be provided at registration desk.
