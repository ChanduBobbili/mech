PPT PRESENTATION

Are you interested in giving seminars? Powerpoint is a tool you can use to communicate your ideas effectively through visual aids that look professionally designed yet are easy make. Get ready for the Presentare event that is being conducted by MECHANO MMXXII and show us your skills!

Rules:

  • Competitors should select a single topic from the given topics and they are given time to explain the breadth and significance of their topic.
  • Presentations are to commence from the stage.
  • Presentations must be made by memory; notes may not be used.
  • At every level of the competition, each competitor will be assessed on the three judging criteria listed below. Please note that each criterion is equally weighted and has an emphasis on audience.
    • Communication
    • Comprehension
    • Engagement
  • The decision of the panel is final.

Topics:

  • Biomechanics
  • Nuclear vs Conventional Submarines
  • Human Resource - Bane or Bane?
  • Science and Technology for Sustainable Future
  • Idea of Atmanirbhar Bharat

Registration fee: 300/- per person
For participants who are interested to participate in multiple events, surprise discounts will be provided at registration desk.
